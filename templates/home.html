Who We Are

We are 8 Computer Science students currently studying in Trinity College Dublin. We set out to find metrics in order to measure a software team’s productivity. You can read more about us individually on our About page.

Our Goal

We aim to not only offer how proactive team members are within an organisation, but to also offer insights into how productive a team is as a whole.

How We Do It

We measure standard tracking measures offered by other software tracking companies. These can be found in Standard Metrics. We more importantly offer a way of tracking team performance through Productivity Measurements.
